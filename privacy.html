資料安全 Data Security

我們實施業界標準的安全措施來保護您的資訊，包括： We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • 對敏感系統的存取控制和身份驗證 Access controls and authentication for sensitive systems

我們僅在必要時保留您的個人資訊，以實現本隱私權政策中描述的目的，除非法律要求或允許更長的保留期限。當不再需要您的資訊時，我們將安全地刪除或匿名化處理。 We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.
